1. **Transaction ID**: A unique identifier for each transaction.
2. **Timestamp**: Date and time of the transaction.
3. **Customer ID**: An identifier for the customer making the purchase (if available).
4. **Product ID**: The identifier of the product(s) purchased.
5. **Quantity Purchased**: The quantity of each product purchased in the transaction.
6. **Price Paid**: The total price paid for the transaction.
7. **Channel**: The sales channel through which the transaction occurred (e.g., online, in-store).
8. **Region/Store ID**: Identifier for the region or store where the transaction took place.
9. **Geo Position (Lat/Long)**: The geographic coordinates of the transaction location.
10. **Payment Method**: The method of payment used for the transaction.
11. **Promotions/Discounts Applied**: Any discounts or promotions applied to the transaction.
12. **Customer Segment**: A classification of customers based on demographic or behavioral characteristics.
13. **Transaction Type**: Indicates the type of transaction (e.g., regular purchase, return, exchange).
14. **Transaction Status**: Indicates the status of the transaction (e.g., completed, pending).
15. **Salesperson ID**: Identifier for the salesperson involved in the transaction (if applicable).


**Data Types**:
- **Transaction ID**: Alphanumeric string or integer.
- **Timestamp**: Date/time data type.
- **Customer ID**: Alphanumeric string or integer.
- **Product ID**: Alphanumeric string or integer.
- **Quantity Purchased**: Numeric (integer or float).
- **Price Paid**: Numeric (integer or float).
- **Channel**: Categorical variable.
- **Region/Store ID**: Alphanumeric string or integer.
- **Geo Position (Lat/Long)**: Float data type for latitude and longitude.
- **Payment Method**: Categorical variable.
- **Promotions/Discounts Applied**: Categorical or Boolean variable.
- **Customer Segment**: Categorical variable.
- **Transaction Type**: Categorical variable.
- **Transaction Status**: Categorical variable.
- **Salesperson ID**: Alphanumeric string or integer.
